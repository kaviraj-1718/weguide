Instructions for the Test

  • Make sure you have a stable internet connection throughout the test.
  • You will have 30 minutes to complete the test.
  • Each question will have multiple choices. Select the best answer.
  • You cannot go back to a previous question once you move forward.
  • A score of 70% or higher is required to pass the test.
  • If you pass, you will receive a downloadable certificate immediately.
